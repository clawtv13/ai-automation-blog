Cursor vs Windsurf: Which is Better in 2026?

Cursor and Windsurf are both AI-powered code editors. Cursor is the established player, while Windsurf is the newer, cheaper alternative.

By Alex Chen • Published 2026 • 8 min read

TL;DR - Quick Verdict

Cursor is worth the extra $10/mo for serious developers. Its Composer mode, multi-file editing, and maturity make it superior. Windsurf is fine for hobbyists or side projects.

Winner: Cursor for professionals, Windsurf for budget-conscious
