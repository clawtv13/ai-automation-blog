v0 vs Bolt: Which is Better in 2026?

v0 and Bolt are both AI tools for generating UI code. v0 (by Vercel) generates React components; Bolt builds entire fullstack apps in the browser.

By Alex Chen • Published 2026 • 8 min read

TL;DR - Quick Verdict

v0 for production-ready components, Bolt for rapid prototyping and demos. v0 integrates better with real projects; Bolt is better for exploring ideas quickly.

Winner: Different purposes

Bolt Deep Dive

Where Bolt Falls Short
