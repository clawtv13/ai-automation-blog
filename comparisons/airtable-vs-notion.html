Airtable vs Notion: Which is Better in 2026?

Airtable and Notion both offer database functionality, but with different strengths. Airtable is database-first; Notion is docs-first with database features.

By Alex Chen • Published 2026 • 8 min read

TL;DR - Quick Verdict

Airtable for serious data work and automations. Notion for lightweight databases integrated with docs and wikis. Most solo founders start with Notion and graduate to Airtable.

Winner: Airtable for data-heavy work

Which Should You Choose?

Use Notion if databases are secondary to docs. Use Airtable if data management and automation are core to your workflow.

✅ Choose Airtable if:

  • Flexible database with automations
  • You prioritize spreadsheet + database hybrid
  • Budget: Free + $10/mo+

✅ Choose Notion if:

  • All-in-one workspace for solopreneurs
  • You prioritize extremely flexible
  • Budget: Free + $8/mo+

Can you use both? Absolutely. Many solo founders use Airtable for database and Notion for note-taking.
